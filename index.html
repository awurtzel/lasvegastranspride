Las Vegas TransPride

Introducing Ourselves

Las Vegas TransPride works on bringing Pride, Awareness, Remembrance, Celebration, Empowerment and Support to the Trans* Expansive community with little or no charge to the community. We strive on bringing community together in a safe place and offering various activities throughout the year that can bring the community together, and working alongside of the larger LGBTQ community.

This year has been a very challenging year, especially due to covid-19. We sincerely hope you are staying safe during this time.

Since the state is slowly allowing businesses to safely come together, while adhering to the social distancing guidelines, we will start opening our space for some events throughout the year, while maintaining social distant guidelines. We hope you will want to work with us to make our community a more robust and vibrant community in supporting each other on our individual journeys.

Just because we have not been very visible in the community this year, we still provide service to help individuals in our community. We have been working on an individual basis to provide services for anyone requiring an extra hand. Some of the services we provide on an appointment only basis are:
Help with filling out the name change documents; we provide the paperwork.
Providing Chest binders to individuals desiring a masculine chest appearance.
Individual spiritual pastoral counseling.
Providing Healthcare Treatment Advocacy.
     -Accompanying individuals on hospital visits
    - Accompanying individuals to medical providers appointments
Providing frozen meals and some donated food items to anyone needing food assistance.
Hosting the Annual Transgender Day of Remembrance.
Providing activities throughout the year to help bring individuals together.

Please feel free to contact us, if you have any questions about our services, and how we may be able to help you on your journey. If you would like to be a part of our team, please contact us! We look forward to hearing from you soon!

Therefore, every donation is important to keep the cost down to the community. Every donation makes a difference, and no donation is too small. We hope you will consider becoming a regular donor to Las Vegas TransPride, so we may continue to keep our various forms of outreach into our community.
